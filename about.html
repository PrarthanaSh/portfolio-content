About me

When I engage in designing for impact, I put the environment first in my sensitization of values. Belonging to a generation that saw revolutionary transformations, I believe I am responsible for both the ecological imbalance and for restoring it. It generates a juxtaposition of generating impactful designs for reducing adverse impact on the environment.

I am a believer of the tiny-karma philosophy. This is derived from the Hindu epic called Mahabharata. "Every little action adds up to a bigger outcome. Nothing ever goes fully unnoticed." This thought motivates me to be mindful about my habits, routine and my work ethics.
