Designing for impact

Like most design students, there was a time when I wanted to create impact. Today, I aim to reduce impact through my design- the impact on our environment.

This portfolio speaks about who I am as a person but also about the work I do, the values I represent and the myriad number of things that interest me. It is a hunble attempt in portraying my efforts towards my professional growth and my transition from a seed into a little sapling.
